Freelance program

                              Freelance programming is the process of contracting your
                             coding and application development 
                            skills to clients. Freelance programmers are their own bosses,
                             meaning they pay their taxes, choose their clients, set their hours and manage
                              their workspace.

As a freelance programmer, you can write code for websites,
 software, mobile apps and any other type of computing application.
  You communicate with clients to determine their needs, then complete 
  the project from start to finish.

Some programming professionals work on freelance projects 
part time while still working at a full-time programming job.
 Others are independent contractors who use their coding skills to 
 earn an income. Freelance programmers have complete control over the projects
  they accept, so they decide how many hours per week they can
   dedicate to freelancing.
